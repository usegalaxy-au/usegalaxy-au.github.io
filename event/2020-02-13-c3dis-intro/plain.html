Introduction to the Galaxy workflow tool @ C3DIS 2020 Melbourne

training

From 2020-03-18 to 2020-03-18 - Add to your Calendar

CSIRO along with the Queensland Facility for Advanced Bioinformatics (QFAB) and Melbourne Bioinformatics will be conducting a workshop on Introduction to the Galaxy workflow tool as part of the Collaborative Conference on Computational and Data Intensive Science 2020 (C3DIS 2020) on March the 18th 2020 from 9:30am until 12:30pm.

The C3DIS 2020 conference will be held at the Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre from March 16th to 20th 2020.

Details of the workshop can be found at: http://www.c3dis.com/4025. Registration for the workshop is via http://www.c3dis.com/registration
