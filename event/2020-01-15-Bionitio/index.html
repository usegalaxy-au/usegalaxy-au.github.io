WEBINAR: Bionitio - building better bioinformatics tools (with batteries included)

training

From 2020-01-20 to 2020-01-20 - Add to your Calendar

The Australian Biocommons is presenting a webinar on the 20th of January from 1 - 2pm AEDT (12 - 1pm AEST) for bioinformatics developers on a tool called Bionitio that automates the process of starting a new bioinformatics software project.

Details and registration information of the webinar can be found at [https://www.biocommons.org.au/events/bionitio-2019](https://www.biocommons.org.au/events/bionitio-2019) ---
