2021 Galaxy Community Conference (GCC2021) - Poster/Demo abstracts due 14th June

»
May 31, 2021
conference

The 2021 Galaxy Community Conference is almost upon us. GCC2021 will be held 28 June through July 10.

June 14: Poster Abstracts are due

We welcome poster/demo submissions that use, implement or extend the Galaxy platform and ecosystem, including integrating and analyzing datasets in any research area, enhancing reproducibility in computational analyses, and fostering collaboration in scientific workflow development

Poster/Demos will be presented live at Remo.co tables and will not be limited to a single large PDF. They are an opportunity to fully communicate your work.

Submit your abstract now.
