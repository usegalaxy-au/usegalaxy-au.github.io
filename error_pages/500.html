Galaxy Australia Outage Notification - 09/06/2021 - 11/06/2021

The Galaxy Australia service will be completely offline for a 3 day window from the 9th June 2021 until the 11th June 2021. Our hosting service provider is undergoing a shutdown during this period for essential maintenance and we have been notified that it will effect our service as well.

  • No new jobs will be accepted from 17:00 on 8 June 2021 AEST.
  • The scheduled outage will then occur from 09:00 9 June - 17:00 11 June 2021 AEST.

If you have planned work during this outage, then please contact the support team as soon as possible, to explore options for your ongoing analyses (help@genome.edu.au).
